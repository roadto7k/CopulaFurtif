core.DAO.Crypto.Crypto_history module

core.DAO.Crypto.Crypto_history.import_crypto_data(symbol: str, period: str = '1y', interval: str = '1d')[source]

Importe l’historique des prix d’une cryptomonnaie depuis Yahoo Finance.

Parameters:

  • symbol – Symbole de la crypto (ex: ‘BTC-USD’, ‘ETH-USD’)

  • period – Durée de l’historique (‘1d’, ‘5d’, ‘1mo’, ‘3mo’, ‘6mo’, ‘1y’, ‘2y’, ‘5y’, ‘10y’, ‘ytd’, ‘max’)

  • interval – Granularité (‘1m’, ‘2m’, ‘5m’, ‘15m’, ‘30m’, ‘1h’, ‘1d’, ‘5d’, ‘1wk’, ‘1mo’, ‘3mo’)

Returns:

DataFrame contenant l’historique des prix
